Custom Clearance

  • Customs documentation can often be a tedious and complex process for exporters and importers. With extensive knowledge of customs procedures and documentation, SYS's expert team is here to assist you every step of the way.
  • We help classify commodities based on Harmonized System Codes (HS Codes) and guide our clients through the required documentation to ensure smooth and hassle-free shipment clearance.
  • SYS also liaises with various government authorities, including:
  • ADC Department
  • FSSAI
  • Spice Board
  • Health Department
  • Pytho Department
  • Additionally, we work with multiple consulates to legalize documents as needed, ensuring a seamless process for your shipments.
