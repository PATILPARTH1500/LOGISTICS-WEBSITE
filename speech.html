Managing Director's Message Mr.Yogesh

  • Welcome to SYS Shipping & Logistics Pvt Ltd.
  • Our story is one of growth, resilience, and the unwavering pursuit of excellence. From our beginnings to becoming a trusted partner in the global shipping and logistics industry, every milestone we’ve reached reflects the dedication and passion of our team.
  • I didn’t always envision myself as an entrepreneur. Born into a middle-class family, I began my career in the Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) sector. However, my true calling emerged when I pursued a Diploma in Export & Import Management, which ignited my passion for the shipping and logistics industry. That turning point, combined with a Master’s in Marketing Management from Mumbai University, propelled me into a journey that I could have never imagined.
  • In 2004, I started as a Documentation Clerk in a shipping line, where I gained valuable experience across various functions, including Documentation, Customer Service, and Marketing. Over time, I recognized the need for innovation and flexibility in the logistics industry. With the encouragement of my clients and the unwavering support of my family, I founded what is now SYS Shipping & Logistics Pvt Ltd in 2012, originally named “Seair Global Logistics.”
  • Today, we are proud to serve our clients with unparalleled service, tailored solutions, and a commitment to delivering seamless logistics experiences globally. We continue to evolve, always driven by our mission to provide value, integrity, and excellence in every service we offer.
  • Thank you for being a part of this incredible journey.
