MD's Biography Mr. Yogesh Shelar

  • Myself Mr. Yogesh Shelar founder & CEO of “SYS Shipping & Logistics Pvt Ltd Pvt Ltd” have more than 15 years of experience in Shipping & Logistics Industry.
  • Born in middle class family, did not have any vision of starting own business. After completion of graduation in Commerce, start working in a Business Process Outsourcing Industry (BPO) but this job failed to create any enthusiasm in my life to continue the work.
  • Hence joined Diploma in Export & Import Management which became turning point of my life & later did Master in Marketing Management from Mumbai University.
  • In 2004, I decided to switch the Industry & got an opportunity to work as “Documentation Clerk” in Shipping Line & then began journey of my Career.
  • I handled various profiles like Documentation, Customer Service & Marketing etc.
  • In 2012, there was recommendation from the Clients to start my own business & with the support of Family, I have decided & start an organization with the name of “Seair Global Logistics” which later converted to “SYS Shipping & Logistics Pvt Ltd Pvt Ltd”
